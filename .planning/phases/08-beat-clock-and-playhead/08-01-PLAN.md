<objective>
Extract the beat-position formula duplicated in canvasEngine.ts and audioEngine.ts into a single pure module — beatClock.ts. Both engines are then refactored to call getCurrentBeat(bpm) instead of repeating the inline arithmetic.

Purpose: ANIM-15 requires beat position to be derivable at RAF call time from a single, non-React source. Centralising the formula makes it trivially importable by the playhead RAF loop added in Plan 02.
Output: beatClock.ts (new), canvasEngine.ts and audioEngine.ts (refactored imports).
</objective>
